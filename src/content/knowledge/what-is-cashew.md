Cashew is a tropical evergreen tree scientifically known as *Anacardium occidentale*.

It produces two valuable products:

- Cashew Apple
- Cashew Kernel

Cambodia has become one of the world's leading producers of premium-quality cashews.

## Why are Cashews Important?

Cashews are:

- Nutritious
- Delicious
- High in healthy fats
- Rich in minerals
