# Purpose

This document defines the overall information architecture of the Mato Cashew website.

It serves as the blueprint for:

- Navigation
- URL Structure
- Content Organization
- Internal Linking
- SEO Hierarchy
- Future Expansion

---

# Website Vision

The Mato Cashew website is designed as a digital platform rather than a traditional company website.

The platform consists of four core pillars:

• Brand Website

• Product Catalog

• Knowledge Hub

• Wholesale & Export Platform

# Design Principles

Keep navigation simple.

Maximum three clicks to any content.

Educate before selling.

Content first.

SEO by design.

Accessibility by default.

Bilingual by default.

---

# Success Criteria

Users should easily find information.

Importers should quickly access product specifications.

Consumers should discover products naturally.

Google should clearly understand the site hierarchy.

---

# Conclusion

The Information Architecture provides the long-term structural foundation for the Mato Cashew platform and ensures that future growth remains organized, scalable, and user-friendly.
